Ruihuang Li

I am a second-year PhD student in Computer Science at the The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (HKPolyU), working with Prof. Lei Zhang at the Department of Computing. I received my master degree from Tianjin University, the College of Intelligence and Computing, working with Prof. Qinghua Hu and Prof. Changqing Zhang.

I also work with Prof. Xu Jia as a research intern in Hua Wei Noah's Ark Lab.

Research
